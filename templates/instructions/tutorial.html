Comprehensive Website Tutorial

This page explains every major feature and function for both customers and restaurant owners.

For Customers

  • Browse Restaurants: Explore all available restaurants and filter by cuisine, rating, or location.
  • View Menus: See detailed menus, item descriptions, and prices for each restaurant.
  • Add to Cart: Add items to your cart, group by restaurant, and review your order before checkout.
  • Checkout & Payment: Choose your delivery method (pickup or delivery), enter your details, and pay securely.
  • Order Tracking: Track your order status in real time and receive notifications when your food is ready or dispatched.
  • Leave Reviews: Rate your experience and leave feedback for restaurants.
  • Account Management: Update your profile, view order history, and manage saved addresses.

For Restaurant Owners

  • Register & Set Up: Add your restaurant, complete your profile, and set your business hours.
  • Menu Management: Add, edit, or remove menu items and organize them by category.
  • Order Management: View, accept, and update orders as they come in. Mark orders as ready or dispatched.
  • Promotions & Analytics: Create promo codes, view sales analytics, and monitor customer feedback.
  • Staff Management: Invite staff, assign roles, and manage permissions.
  • Support & Help: Access help resources and contact support from your dashboard.
