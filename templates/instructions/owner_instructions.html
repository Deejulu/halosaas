Instructions for Restaurant Owners

Welcome! This page will guide you through everything you need to know to manage your restaurant on our platform.

  1. Register your restaurant: Use the "Add Restaurant" button on your dashboard to begin.
  2. Complete your profile: Fill in all required details, upload your logo, and set your opening hours.
  3. Add menu items: Go to "Manage Menu" to add, edit, or remove dishes. Use categories for better organization.
  4. Order management: View and process incoming orders from the "Orders" section. Update order status as you prepare and dispatch.
  5. Promotions: Create promo codes and special offers to attract more customers.
  6. Analytics: Track your sales, popular items, and customer feedback in the "Analytics" dashboard.
  7. Staff management: Invite and manage staff roles for your restaurant.
  8. Support: Access help and contact support from your dashboard if you need assistance.
