How to Set Up Your Restaurant

  1. Step 1: Add Your Restaurant
    Click the Add Restaurant button and fill out all required fields: name, contact info, address, opening hours, and images.
  2. Step 2: Complete Basic Info
    Enter a clear restaurant name, phone, email, and a short description. This helps customers find and trust your business.
  3. Step 3: Upload Images
    Add a logo and banner image. Good images make your restaurant stand out.
  4. Step 4: Set Payment Methods
    Choose all payment options your restaurant accepts. More options make it easier for customers to pay.
  5. Step 5: Save and Preview
    Click Create Restaurant to save. You can preview your restaurant page and make edits anytime.
  6. Step 6: Edit Your Menu
    After creating your restaurant, go to the Menu Manager to add dishes, prices, and descriptions.
  7. Step 7: Manage Orders & Staff
    Use the dashboard to view orders, manage staff, and update your restaurant profile.
Tip: You can always return to this help page for guidance. If you need more help, contact support from your dashboard.
